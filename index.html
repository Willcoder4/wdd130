Nigeria

Nigeria is a populous and diverse federal republic in West Africa, known as the "Giant of Africa" for its vast population, rich culture, abundant natural resources like oil, and varied geography ranging from desert to rainforest. It is home to over 250 ethnic groups and languages, though English is the official language, and its capital is Abuja, with Lagos serving as the economic hub. The country's history includes a colonial past, independence in 1960, and a path toward stable democracy.
